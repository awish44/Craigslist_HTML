Does number of listings affect price?

Analysis completed: 2/14/2019



This scatterplot is grouped-by the number of apartment listings per neighborhood and is reflecting the average price. My goal was to understand if price affects the number of listings in a neighborhood I am interested in. I was curious to understand rent by neighborhood and availability.
Based on this dataset, you can see that North Beach currently has highest # of apartments available and the rent for a 2 bedroom drives the mean rent of all neighborhoods. Pac Heights 1 apartment listing is the most expensive at $3500.
My follow-up is to deep-dive into the amentiies and size variances from North Beach and Pac Heights apts.
