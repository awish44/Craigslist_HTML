Neighborhood Analysis

I took a different and more visually appealing approach to this analysis.
Goal of exploring different plots and finding unique ways to make data digestible for any audience.


Which neighborhood has the most listings?

Analysis completed: 2/13/2019



Word Cloud increase the size of a word baed on the frequency of its occurance in a data-set. This example portrays which neighborhood has the most apartment listings on Craigslist. I leveraged this visual as a starting point to understand which neighborhood I should dig deeper into. Based on the parameters I set and the 28 apartment listings that were returned, Lower Nob Hill appears to be have the widest variety for what I am looking for.

Prices vs Number of Listings

Analysis completed: 2/13/2019



Created a violin plot that groups Neighborhoods and compares prices against number of listings. The lines in each plot represent the number of listings. Based on this data, majority of Lower Nob Hill's listings prices raneg between $3K-$3.5K. An outlier in this plot would be the Nob Hill listing that is extremely low compared to other listings.
